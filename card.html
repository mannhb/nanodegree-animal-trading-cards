Cheetah

The fastest land animal in the world, a cheetah can reach a speed of 60mph in just 3 seconds.

The fur of a cheetah is tan in color with round, solid black spots. Long and lanky with a deep chest that contains large lungs, a cheetah can easily reach high speeds to hunt down prey. The name cheetah originates from the Sanskrit term Chitraka, which means speckled.
